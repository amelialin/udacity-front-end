Common Octopus

The octopus is a master of disguise, and can change to match a wide variety of surroundings.

  • Scientific Name: Octopus vulgaris
  • Kingdom: Animalia
  • Phylum: Mollusca
  • Class: Cephalopoda

Octopi have eight arms and a beak. They have no skeleton, allowing them to squeeze through extremely tight places. Octopi are among the most intelligent animals.
